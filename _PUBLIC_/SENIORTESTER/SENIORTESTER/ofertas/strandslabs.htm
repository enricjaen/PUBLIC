QA Engineer

Strands Labs - Barcelona Area, Spain (Barcelona y alrededores, España)

Descripción del empleo

We are looking forfor an enthusiastic and professional quality assurance engineer with outstanding technical abilities.


The position will report to the COO of the company.

If you are looking to join a energetic and highly skilled team of great people, are passionate about what you are doing, like working with clients and have excellent technical skills, this might be the job for you.

Responsibilities for the position include:

  • Quality assurance testing of Strands software (web and mobile)
  • Responsible for identifying, logging, tracking, and escalating bugs
  • Manage defect reporting for external customers
  • Verifying fixes and regression testing
  • QA documentation for internal and external customers
  • Write, maintain, and execute test cases (manual and automated) based on product specifications
  • Be an active partner in requirement and specification review with a QA perspective
  • Develop automated functional and regression tests using Java and Selenium (Webdriver, Grid, PageObjects)
  • Setup test environments and test data

Experiencia y aptitudes deseadas

The successful candidate will have:

  • B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent with minimum 5 years experience
  • Must be fluent in English, both written and spoken
  • 3 years experience with quality assurance testing processes and tools
  • Experience with SQL and relational databases
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to dynamically prioritize between conflicting priorities and multiple projects
  • Great attitude and team player.
  • Strong initiative with attention to details

 

Candidates with the following, additional skills will be strongly preferred

  • Experience in testing mobile applications
  • Experience developing web applications with Java, JavaScript, CSS, jQuery
  • Prior experience with TestNG
  • Experience in testing web accessibility
  • Previous experience with the Financial Industry (Banking, Insurance)
  • Previous experience with a Start-up is a plus

 

Descripción de la empresa

Strands Labs powers online personalization by providing solutions within the financial and recommendation industries.

Strands Finance is a leading provider of personal finance management solutions. It enables financial institutions to better engage their customers while helping users develop effective money management habits.
