Software Development Engineer in Test

Job description

Do you want to explore how personal, enterprise and consumer information can drive a new breed of immersive experiences? The FAST team has delivered search technology to the most demanding companies around the world and power information access in SharePoint and Exchange. By joining the FAST team you will be working on search, Big Data, machine learning, massive scale, cloud service delivery and app experiences across all devices. You will join a team that is incredible passionate and you get the opportunity to impact the daily life of billions of people around the world.

Our SDETs have a passion for quality and a strong motivation for delivering highly valuable products and solutions. We have a strong focus on designing and developing automated test systems to validate functionality, performance, stability and security of enterprise class products installed by millions of customers. We also have a strong focus on usability and user experience assessments and develop and use scientific methods to improve product usability. Measuring product quality and fitness is highly challenging and requires ingenuity, innovation and excellent engineering skills.

You will be part of a truly global team with one of the world’s largest and most successful software development companies, and you will have an excellent opportunity to challenge yourself and grow in a highly energized and innovative work environment.

Responsibilities
•Participate in measuring and driving product quality from inception to release.
•Responsibility for the quality of selected product features.
•Improve and extend test frameworks and automated test sets.

Qualifications
We will give preference to candidates that possesses one or more of the following qualifications:
•B.Sc. or higher degree in Computer Science or similar field with good results.
•0-2 years of relevant experience.
•Experience with development in languages like C# or C++.
•Strong problem solving skills.
•Working understanding of techniques for testing software.
•Working understanding of usability and techniques for assessing product usability.
•Passion for software quality and for advancing testing as en engineering discipline.
•Ability to work and participate in a team with open and collaborative style of communications.
•Strong oral and written English.
